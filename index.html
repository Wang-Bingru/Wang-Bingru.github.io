Competition Experience

Competition


Last year, after layers of selection, my team and I participated in the competition on behalf of the college. After several months, we designed the appearance, mechanism, circuit board, control program and so on of the cotton picking robot from scratch. Because of the Covid-19 pandemic, we could only participate in the online competition, and finally won the Best Video award (the first prize of the international competition according to the school rules). You are welcome to browse the fruits of our labor below!

It was because of this competition that my interest in scientific research was aroused. I like to be able to sink my heart into one thing, delve into problems and propose solutions. At the same time, during the preparation for the competition, while completing the heavy tasks, I was honed to bear hardships and have the ability of coordination.
